Intelligence Briefing
Week of 14 March 2026
Geopolitical headwinds dominate. The Gulf crisis following the Iran conflict has sent Brent crude to $90/bbl, pressuring Pakistan's import bill and current account. The SBP held rates at 10.5% but T-bill yields jumped 39 bps to 11% — signalling the market expects further tightening. The Rupee hit a fresh record low against the Dollar.

Capital protection is paramount this fortnight. The playbook is clear: maximise allocation to gold, PKR-denominated fixed income instruments, and Shariah-compliant income funds. Reduce or avoid direct PSX equity exposure during this volatile period. International REITs and defence stocks remain structurally attractive but carry currency risk for PKR-based investors.
